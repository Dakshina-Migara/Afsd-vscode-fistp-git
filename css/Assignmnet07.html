About ACPT

ACPT (Academy of Computer Programming and Training) is Sri Lanka’s leading institute for practical, career-focused education. We empower students with real-world skills in software development, digital marketing, logistics, and office administration to help them secure well-paying jobs in today’s competitive market.

We began as a passionate software development team and quickly realized a major problem in the industry — a shortage of skilled professionals, not degrees. That’s why ACPT was founded: to close the skill gap and create confident, job-ready professionals who can contribute to any company from day one.

Today, ACPT is powered by a network of industry experts, developers, marketers, and educators. Together, our mission is simple — to build the strongest, most employable workforce in Sri Lanka.
